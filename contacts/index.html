Shaping Future Leaders

Glory International Academy is a leading educational institution dedicated to shaping future leaders through quality education. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where students can excel academically, socially, and personally.

We are a Nursery and Primary Academy located at Katooke Nabweru, with a curriculum that is learner-based and integrated with the International Primary Curriculum (IPC).

We engage our pupils in leisure activities like swimming and co-curricular activities like MDD (Music, Dance, and Drama) to ensure holistic development.

We also train computer literacy to our pupils to make them more practical in this dynamic digital world.
